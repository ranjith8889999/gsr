Our Journey

A timeline of dedication, service, and community development

Beginning of Service

2023

Ganta Shiva Reddy began his journey as a standing corporator with a vision to transform our community through inclusive development and active citizen engagement.

First Kotideepotsavam

2024

Organized the inaugural Kotideepotsavam, bringing together thousands of devotees who lit diyas with devotion and faith. This event became a symbol of unity and spiritual awakening in our community.

Reddys Community Vanabhojanalu

2024

Successfully conducted Vanabhojanalu for the Reddy community, fostering stronger bonds and celebrating our rich cultural heritage with traditional gatherings and community feasts.

Second Kotideepotsavam

2025

Organized an even bigger and more successful Kotideepotsavam, with increased participation and community involvement, creating a festival of lights that illuminated hearts and homes.

Continued Community Service

2025

Conducted the second edition of Reddys Community Vanabhojanalu, further strengthening community bonds and preserving our cultural traditions for future generations.

Signature Events

Bringing our community together through memorable celebrations

Featured

Kotideepotsavam

2024 & 2025 5000+ Participants

An extraordinary celebration where thousands of devotees gathered to light diyas, creating a spectacular festival of lights. This event has become a cornerstone of our community's spiritual and cultural identity, bringing together people from all walks of life in a beautiful display of faith and unity.

Highlights:

  • Thousands of diyas lit simultaneously
  • Community prayers and cultural programs
  • Free distribution of diyas and prasadam
  • Traditional music and devotional songs
Popular

Reddys Community Vanabhojanalu

2024 & 2025 1000+ Members

A grand gathering of the Reddy community that celebrates our rich cultural heritage through traditional Vanabhojanalu. This event strengthens community bonds and provides an opportunity for families to come together, share traditional cuisine, and preserve our ancestral customs for future generations.

Highlights:

  • Traditional community feast
  • Cultural performances and folk arts
  • Elder's blessings and wisdom sharing
  • Networking and community bonding
